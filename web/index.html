Direction 2 · learned predictive topology

A learned cost-to-go replaces the hand-tuned knob.

Why learning wins

The paper steers topology with a hand-tuned potential field ζ. Drag the slider: its energy wobbles (fragile). The learned value model V̂(state) predicts remaining mission energy and needs no knob — a flat, lower line.

Optimality gap vs a brute-force oracle (N=5). Every per-round matcher — including the ACO/ABC/Cuckoo/Egyptian-Vulture baselines — clusters at the myopic level; only the learned lookahead closes the gap.
